The Wonderful and Amazing

James Morrison

The maestro, James Morrison, plays us some sweet, sweet tunes on his horn.

This is why I love James Morrison and why you will too:

  1. He is an Aussie, so he has a beautiful accent.

  2. Not only can he play trumpet to a world class standard, but he can also play:
    • Trombone
    • Alto Sax
    • Tenor Sax
    • Piano
    • And many more...
    All to a professional standard!!

  3. He has worked with Schagerl to produce a new hybrid rotary/piston-valve instrument called "raven".

  4. He has brilliant covers of Waltzing Mathilda and Nobody Does it Better.

  5. In his wonderful big band album Snappy Too, he plays every single instrument excluding the drum kit! It's definately one to listen to!
